Green Line Elevator

Greenline Elevators is headquartered in Cape Town, the manufacturing capital of South Africa. Along with the years, Greenline has established regional office and service network in all over Africa. We take pride in providing one-stop solution from pre-sale service to after-sale service for the customers around the world.

Greenline Elevators is a company that takes pride in offering customized elevator solutions designed to match the distinct requirements of its customers. With more than 15 years of expertise in the field, Greenline Elevators has established a strong track record for delivering high-quality offerings. The company's team of seasoned experts guarantees punctual elevator installation, maintenance, and repair services to ensure elevators have optimal uptime, all while prioritizing energy efficiency and eco-friendliness..
